Preparation of Yellow Tea

Ingredients :

  • Fresh, cold water
  • Yellow tea leaves (specifically processed yellow tea leaves)

Preparation :

  1. Boil Fresh Water :

    • Begin by boiling fresh, cold water. The quality of the water can affect the taste of the tea, so consider using filtered or spring water.

  2. Preheat Teapot or Teacup :

    • Pour a small amount of hot water into the teapot or teacup to warm it. Swirl the water around, then discard it. This helps maintain the temperature of the tea during brewing.

  3. Measure Yellow Tea Leaves :

    • Use about one to two teaspoons of yellow tea leaves per 8 ounces of water. Place the yellow tea leaves into a tea infuser or directly into the teapot.

  4. Pour Hot Water Over the Tea :

    • Once the water has reached a temperature between 160째F to 185째F (71째C to 85째C), pour it over the yellow tea leaves. Avoid using boiling water, as it can affect the delicate flavors of yellow tea

  5. Steep the Tea :

    • Allow the yellow tea to steep for 3 to 5 minutes. The steeping time can be adjusted based on your preference for a stronger or milder flavor. Yellow tea generally requires a slightly longer steeping time compared to green tea.

  6. Strain or Remove Tea Leaves :

    • After steeping, remove the yellow tea leaves or tea bag to prevent over-brewing. Yellow tea leaves are typically more oxidized than green tea leaves but less than black tea leaves.

  7. Optional Additions :

    • Yellow tea is often enjoyed on its own due to its delicate and nuanced flavors. However, you can add honey or a slice of lemon if desired. Milk is generally not added to yellow tea.

  8. Stir and Enjoy :

    • Stir the tea gently to mix in any added ingredients. Allow the yellow tea to cool slightly before sipping to fully appreciate its subtle and complex flavors.

Remember that the recommended water temperature and steeping time may vary depending on the specific type of yellow tea you're using, so it's a good idea to check the instructions provided by the tea manufacturer. Enjoy your soothing cup of yellow tea!
