Preparation of Dandelion Tea

Ingredients :

  • Dandelion leaves (fresh or dried)
  • Water
  • Optional: honey lemon for flavor

Preparation :

  1. Wash the dandelion leaves thoroughly if using fresh ones.
  2. Boil water in a pot.
  3. Add the dandelion leaves to the boiling water.
  4. Let it steep for about 5-10 minutes.
  5. Strain the tea into a cup.
  6. Add honey or lemon if desired.
  7. Enjoy your dandelion tea!
