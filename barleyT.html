Preparation of Barley Tea

Ingredients :

  • 1 cup pearl Barley
  • 6 cups water
  • Optional: sweetener(honey,sugar,stevia) to taste
  • Optional: lemon slices for garnish

Preparation :

  1. Rinse the pearl barley under cold water to remove any dirt or debris.
  2. In a medium-sized pot, bring 6 cups of water to a boil.
  3. Add the rinsed pearl barley to the boiling water.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and let the barley simmer for about 20-30 minutes, or until the water takes on a slightly golden hue.
  5. Remove the pot from heat and let it cool for a few minutes.
  6. Strain the barley tea into a pitcher or container using a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth to remove the barley grains.
  7. Allow the tea to cool further or refrigerate until chilled.
  8. Serve the barley tea over ice with optional sweetener and lemon slices if desired.
