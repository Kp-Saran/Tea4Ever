Preparation of Tea

Ingredients :

  • Water
  • Tea leaves or tea bags
  • Sweetener (optional)
  • Milk (optional)

Preparation :

  1. Boil Water :

    • Measure the desired amount of water for your tea.
    • Bring the water to a boil in a kettle or pot.

  2. Warm the Teapot or Teacup (Optional) :

    • Pour a small amount of hot water into the teapot or teacup to warm it. Swirl the water around, then discard it

  3. Add Tea Leaves or Tea Bag:

    • If using loose tea leaves, add the appropriate amount to a teapot or an infuser.
    • If using tea bags, place the tea bag in the teacup.

  4. Pour Hot Water :

    • Pour the hot water over the tea leaves or tea bag. Use the recommended water temperature for the type of tea you are making.

  5. Steep :

    • Allow the tea to steep for the recommended time. This varies depending on the type of tea (e.g., black tea, green tea, herbal tea).

  6. Remove Tea Leaves or Tea Bag :

    • Remove the tea leaves or tea bag once the steeping time is complete to prevent over-brewing and bitterness.

  7. Add Sweetener and Milk (Optional) :

    • If desired, add sweetener (sugar, honey, etc.) and milk to taste. Stir gently.

  8. Enjoy :

    • Your tea is now ready to be enjoyed. Take a moment to savor the aroma and flavor.

Feel free to customize the ingredients and proportions based on your personal preferences. Additionally, the preparation steps may vary depending on the type of tea you are making.
